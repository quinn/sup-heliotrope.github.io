when I try to send. I did manage to find and set the gpgkey configurable
in the .sup/config.yaml:

  :default: 
    :signature: /home/micah/.signature
    :email: micah@riseup.net
    :sendmail: /usr/sbin/sendmail -oem -ti
    :name: micah anderson
