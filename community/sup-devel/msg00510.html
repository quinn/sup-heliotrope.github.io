Hi,
I use a mime-hook like this for inline html:
in ~/.sup/hooks$ cat mime-decode.rb :

 case content_type
 when "text/html"
   `/usr/bin/lynx -dump '#{filename}'`
 end

To view html when hitting enter you could set your browser in ~/.mailcap:

 text/html; firefox %s

best,
/p
